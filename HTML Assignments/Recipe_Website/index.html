Chocolate Fudge

Description

This rich and creamy chocolate fudge is a delightful treat for any occasion. Made with high-quality cocoa powder and sweetened condensed milk, it's perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth.

Ingredients.

  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps.

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the sugar, cocoa powder, and milk. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until the mixture comes to a boil.
  2. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and continue to cook for about 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
  3. Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the butter, vanilla extract, and salt until the butter is melted and the mixture is smooth.
  4. Pour the fudge mixture into a greased 8x8-inch baking dish. Let it cool at room temperature for about 2 hours or until firm.
  5. Once set, cut the fudge into small squares and serve. Enjoy!
